## Noise Due to Thermal Fluctuation

$$
\Delta x_{rms}=\sqrt{\frac{kT}{K}}
$$
where
- $k$ = Boltzmann Constant
- $T$ = [[Temperature]]
- $K$ = spring constant

### Johnson Noise

$$
v_{rms}=\sqrt{4kTRB}
$$
where
- $R$ = [[Resistance]]
- $B$ = [[Bandwidth]] of system
