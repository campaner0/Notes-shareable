Force is a [[Vector]] value that changes the motion of an object when applied. 

According to [[Newton's Second Law]], 
$$\begin{aligned}\vec{F}=m\vec{a}\\
=\frac{d\vec{p}}{dt}
\end{aligned}$$
where
- $\vec{F}$ = force
- $m$ = [[Mass]]
- $\vec{a}$ = [[Acceleration]]
- $\vec{p}$ = [[Momentum]]
