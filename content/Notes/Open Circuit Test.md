An open circuit test is performed on the    [[Voltage]] side of a [[Transformer]] to find the core 
$$\begin{align*}
\vec{Y}_{E}&= G_{C}-jB_{M}\\
&= \frac{1}{R_{C}}+j \frac{1}{X_{M}}
\end{align*}$$

$$
|\vec{Y}_{E}|= \frac{V_{OC}}{I_{OC}}
$$

$$
PF=\cos\theta= \frac{P_{OC}}{V_{OC}I_{OC}}
$$
