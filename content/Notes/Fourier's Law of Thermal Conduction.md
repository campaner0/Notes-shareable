$$\begin{align*}
Q'&= -\kappa A\frac{\Delta T}{L}\\
&= \frac{dQ}{dt}
\end{align*}$$
where
- $Q$ = [[Thermal Energy|Heat]]
- $\kappa$ = [[Thermal Conductivity]]
- $A$ = cross-secitonal [[Area]] 
- $T$ = [[Temperature]]
- $L$ = length
- $t$ = [[Time]]
