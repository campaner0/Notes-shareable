  #math 

The unit tangent vector to a curve is a [[Unit Vector]] that is [[Tangent]] to a curve at a point $t$ such that:
$$
\vec{T}(t)=\frac{\vec{r}\,'(t)}{|\vec{r}\,'(t)|}
$$
where
- $\vec{T}$ = unit tangent vector
- $\vec{r}\,'$ = [[Derivative]] of a [[Vector Function]]
