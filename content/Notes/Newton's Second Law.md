Newton's second law states that the [[Force]] an object experiences is equal to the rate of change of its [[Momentum]]:
$$
\begin{align}
\vec{F}&=\frac{d}{dt}(m\vec v)\\
&=m\vec a\\
\end{align}
$$
where
- $\vec{F}$ = force
- $t$ = [[Time]]
- $m$ = [[Mass]]
- $\vec{v}$ = [[Velocity]]
- $\vec{a}$ = [[Acceleration]]
