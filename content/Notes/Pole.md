A pole is a type of [[Singularity]] in a complex function. The poles of a function $f$ are the [[Zero|Zeros]] of $1/f$ and vice versa. 

>[!example]
>$$
H(s)= \frac{50}{s+50}
$$ has one pole at $j\omega=-50$, 
>where $s$ is [[Complex Frequency]]  and $\sigma=0$
