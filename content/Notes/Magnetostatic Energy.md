The energy stored by an inductor in its mag field is expressed by:
$$
U_B=\frac 12Li^2
$$

$$\begin{align*}
E&= \frac{1}{2}\mu H^{2}\\[5pt]
&= \frac{B^{2}}{2\mu}
\end{align*}$$


Substituting the above equation for inductance and the 
$$
u_B=\frac{U_B}V=\frac{B^2}{2\mu_0}
$$
